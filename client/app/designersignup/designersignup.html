QuickQuery Explanation

QuickQuery is a new system to get editing demands. Once logged on QQ System, requests corresponding your profile will be send to you. You can accept or refuse any proposal. If you accept a request, your profile will be send to the requester for his validation and the job will be yours. You have 48 hours to complete the job and send back the result.
